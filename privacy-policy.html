Privacy Policy

Understand how GrindQ Technologies LLP protects and manages your user data across our sites and applications.

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

At GrindQ Technologies LLP, we prioritize the privacy and security of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, record, and how we utilize it across our platforms, mobile applications (including SSC Ranker Up, HappyFlow, and Tamil Learning App), and our education platforms (Learn Tamil Online).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of user information based on your interactions with our applications and websites:

  • Personal Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, or company name when you request support, submit form enquiries, or sign up for classes.
  • Profile & Subscription Credentials: Account usernames and billing details utilized to access study sets, mock exams, or private tutoring classrooms.
  • Usage Telemetry: Log data including IP addresses, browser specifications, page view cycles, and app interaction counts, processed strictly to diagnose bugs and optimize responsiveness.

2. How We Use Your Information

GrindQ Technologies LLP processes your information strictly for legitimate business objectives, including:

  • Operating, updating, and maintaining our mobile applications and websites.
  • Analyzing application usage to optimize interface flow and fix bugs.
  • Providing ticket resolution and client communication.
  • Processing subscription renewals and class timetables.
  • Preventing fraudulent access and complying with Indian regulatory guidelines.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative security protocols designed to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction of your personal details. However, please note that no internet transmission or digital storage platform can be guaranteed 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our websites utilize cookies to improve user experience, such as maintaining persistent settings (such as dark mode states or logged-in statuses). You retain the choice to decline cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may prevent certain interactive features of our websites from operating correctly.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to external third parties. This does not include trusted third-party providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users (such as cloud hosting or payment processors), so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. User Rights

In accordance with relevant data protection laws, you retain specific rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete details.
  • The right to request deletion of your account records and profile data from our databases.

To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy desk at support@grindq.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

GrindQ Technologies LLP reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
