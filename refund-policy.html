Refund & Cancellation Policy

Review the guidelines regarding subscription cancellations, online class refunds, and transaction credits for GrindQ Technologies LLP.

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing GrindQ Technologies LLP. We strive to provide premium educational preparation modules (SSC Ranker Up), collaborative tools (HappyFlow), interactive language learning utilities (Tamil Learning App), and structured language coaching courses (Learn Tamil Online).

This Refund and Cancellation Policy details the criteria and processes under which transactions may be cancelled, or subscriptions credited.

1. Application Subscription Cancellations

Subscribing to our application tiers (such as mock-test bundles or team boards) is subject to the following billing policies:

  • Monthly Subscription Tiers: You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will remain active until the end of your current paid billing period, and no further charges will be billed. We do not provide prorated refunds for mid-cycle cancellations.
  • Annual Plan Subscriptions: Annual subscription cancellations must be requested within 3 business days of the initial purchase or renewal transaction to be eligible for a full refund, provided no major digital credits or mock exam keys have been generated or utilized.

2. Digital Learning Initiatives & Online Classes

For custom group classes, private tutoring, or coaching services operated via Learn Tamil Online, the following cancellation guidelines apply:

  • Session Rescheduling: Private one-to-one coaching sessions can be rescheduled without penalty if requested at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled class time. Rescheduling requests made within less than 24 hours may result in the session being marked as completed.
  • Class Package Refunds: If you purchase a multi-session language class package and decide to withdraw, you may request a refund for the remaining unused classes within 7 business days of purchase. Completed classes will be billed at the standard single-session rate, and the remainder refunded.

3. Non-Refundable Purchases

The following transactions are strictly non-refundable:

  • One-off mock test key purchases once the test key has been activated or the exam initiated.
  • Downloadable educational study PDFs, grammar charts, or syllabus books.
  • Class fees for group sessions where the user did not attend without prior cancellation notification.

4. Refund Claim Process

To request a cancellation or claim a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Email your request to support@grindq.com.
  2. Include your transaction ID, purchase email address, name of the application or package, and the reason for your refund request.
  3. Our billing desk will review your claim against our terms and respond within 2 business days.

5. Transaction Settlement

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment source (credit card, net banking, UPI, or mobile wallet) within 5 to 7 business days from the date of refund approval, depending on the processing cycles of our banking partner nodes.
